About Me

I am currently a doctor student specializing in artificial intelligence at South China University of Technology (2024~present), under the supervision of Lei Yang. My research interests encompass large language model, data mining, and deep learning.

I graduated from Anhui Normal University in 2021, earning a B.Eng degree in Internet of Things. That same year, I was accepted into the School of Internet at Anhui University, where I ranked first in my major with a total score of 381 (achieving 143 in professional courses). In 2024, I was admitted to South China University of Technology to pursue my doctor degree.

Currently, I am doing further research on LLM privacy and general graph safety. If you are interested in my work, feel free to reach out to me!
